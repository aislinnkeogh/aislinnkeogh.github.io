I'm Aislinn (she/her).

[æʃliːn kiːoʊ]

I'm a PhD student in the Centre for Language Evolution at the University of Edinburgh, supervised by Professor Jennifer Culbertson and Professor Simon Kirby. I use behavioural experiments and computational modelling to study the role of human memory limitations in the evolution of linguistic structure.

My research is funded by the Scottish Graduate School of Social Science (an ESRC doctoral training programme).
